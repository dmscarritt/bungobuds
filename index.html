Shitleft and Shitload Days

  • Prior to the dawning of the fourteenth day of May in the two thousand twenty first year of Bungo Energy, a knowledge had hitherto been bestowed upon the Bungo Buds. For it was on this day the proclamation rang out for the celebration of Shitleft day, and in a short while longer the celebration of Shitload day. The exact determination each year for the commencement of celebration of these two holidays would henceforth be determined as such: Shitleft day shall be made equivalent to the calculation of the calendar date such that the exact number of days remaining until the final day of the current year be equal to two hundred thirty one days. Shitload day shall be the exact same calculation but in the inverse, such that from the first day that begins the current year the calculation should be found that the two hundred thirty first day of the current year in reference to the agreed upon first day mentioned prior shall be celebrated as Shitload day.

Ultimate Xmas

  • It will hereafter be remembered and respected, that during the Jewish celebration of Hanukkah a new and better holiday will be spread amongst the followers of Bungo Energy. Ultimate Xmas (pronounced "Christmas") will the direct response to and celebration of the glory that is the warmth and dominance of Bungo Energy. The defined dates for Ultimate Xmas will be defined by the decided upon dates for Hanukkah each year, so as to maximize its effectiveness.
